Political science is the systematic study of government systems, political behavior, and the distribution of power within societies.
Politics

Topics in political science include government structures, international relations, public policy, political ideologies, comparative politics, and political theory.

Prominent contributors to political science include thinkers like Machiavelli, Rousseau, Marx, and contemporary scholars like Noam Chomsky and Francis Fukuyama.
